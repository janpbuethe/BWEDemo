Official demo page for the paper "A lightweight and robust method for wideband-to-fullband extension of speech"

This demo page highlights applications of the proposed bandwidth extension method both in the coding and vocoding context. The demo samples were extracted from the EARS dataset which is licensed under the CC-NC 4.0 International license. It is recommended to listen with reasonably good headphones.
